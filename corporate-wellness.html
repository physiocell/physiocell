Corporate Wellness


What is Corporate Wellness?

Corporate wellness is any workplace health promotion activity or organizational policy designed to support healthy behavior in the workplace and to improve healthy outcomes.Wellness programs help employees make smart and healthy choices that can reduce health care costs, increase vitality and diminish absenteeism.

Wellness programs include activities such as company-sponsored exercise, educational seminars, tobacco-cessation programs, women oriented programs, health screenings etc. that are designed to help employees eat better, lose weight and improve their overall physical health.

Why do you need Corporate Wellness?

Creating an on-site wellness program is important as the majority of an employee's time is spent at the workplace. Many chronic diseases are preventable. According to the CDC, chronic diseases account for 75 percent of total healthcare costs. They are also the most preventable type of diseases.

Corporate wellness is not just one solution; it is the culmination of many solutions that work together under one strategy.
