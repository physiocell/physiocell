what is Biometric Screening?

Determining one’s current health through clinical process is biometric screening. It states the health of a given population through health indicators. Biometric screen ings provide critical information about both an individual as well as the overall health of an organization

Biometric health screenings should be considered the starting point for any company seeking to reduce health plan costs as they provide an accurate baseline analysis regarding the current health status of the entire group.

75% of health care costs are attributed to chronic conditions that are preventable if diagnosed at an early stage or with a regular check-up.
