What is Lifestyle Induced Disorder Awareness Program?

Lifestyle Disorder means a pattern of individual practices and behavioral choices that are related to elevated health risk, knowingly or unknowingly. Lifestyle Induced Awareness Programs have several factors helping an individual to be aware and lower his health’s risk.

Why do you need this program?

The diseases, which primarily arise from the abnormal lifestyle of a person, are grouped under the term “Lifestyle Induced Disorder”. For working population, the risk of lifestyle disorder looms larger. Therefore, these awareness programs are aimed to find the prevalence and awareness of lifestyle induced disorder related risk factors to enable higher immunity to increased desk time and focus from an employee.
